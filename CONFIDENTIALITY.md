# CONFIDENTIALITY NOTICE

This repository contains confidential research material owned by
Modulith Research CIC.

## What You May Share
- Mathematical proofs (after PI review and approval)
- Paper drafts (after PI review and approval)
- Sanitised experimental data as provided in specs/

## What You May NOT Share
- Any speculation about the underlying implementation
- System parameters beyond those in the specification document
- Raw experimental data not explicitly provided
- Any information about the proprietary codebase
- Details of the routing policy implementation
- Details of the module training procedure

## Paper Submission Policy
All papers must be reviewed and approved by the PI before submission.
The PI has final authority on what information appears in publications.

## Questions
If you are unsure whether something can be shared or published,
ask the PI before proceeding.
